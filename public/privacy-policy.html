Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Delybell Order Sync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Shopify app ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

When you install and use the App, we collect the following information:

  • Shop Information: Your Shopify store domain and basic shop details
  • Order Data: Order information including customer details, shipping addresses, order items, and order status
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens required to access your Shopify store data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To sync orders from your Shopify store to the Delybell delivery management system
  • To process and fulfill delivery orders
  • To provide customer support and technical assistance
  • To improve our services

4. Data Sharing

We share order data with Delybell's delivery management system to process and fulfill deliveries. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. When you uninstall the App, we delete your authentication tokens and stop processing your orders. Historical order data may be retained in Delybell's system as required for delivery fulfillment.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Uninstall the App at any time

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your authentication state. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
